About Me

Hi! I'm Ivy Patterson.

I'm a Front-End Developer with an eye for design and a passion for problem-solving.

I spent a number of years searching for the right career path. I tried out two different college majors, worked as a photographer, a customer service representative, and a bartender. Burnout came all-too quickly, and my pay didn't justify the exhaustion I felt.

I turned to the tech industry in the hopes that it would bring me a sustainable career and more financial stability. What I didn't foresee was just how much I would come to love this work. As a learner and a problem-solver, I feel completely at home in Web Development.

The granny life is the life for me. I love to read, crochet, knit, and hang out with my cats, Winston (left) and Elton (right).

I also have a beloved basset hound named Lucy. She is constantly talking and she WILL eat your food if you don't keep an eye on it. Also sometimes if you do keep an eye on it.
