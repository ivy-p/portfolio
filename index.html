About Me

Hi! I'm Ivy Patterson.

Before I made the leap into Web Development, I studied Interior Design, where I came to appreciate the impact of a well-designed space. The marks of a great design are as follows: functionality and ease, complemented by texture and eye-catching form, with a personalized touch.

Hearing a client's vision and making it come to life is an art form; one I am taking with me into the tech space. I am passionate about creating well-constructed, beautiful spaces, and the impact I can make is that much greater in a virtual environment.

I am a strategic problem-solver, an empathetic listener, and a team-player. I love to code, and most of all, I love to bring ideas to life.

The granny life is the life for me. I love to read, crochet, knit, and hang out with my cats, Winston (left) and Elton (right).

I also have a beloved basset hound named Lucy. She is constantly talking and she WILL eat your food if you don't keep an eye on it. Also sometimes if you do keep an eye on it.
